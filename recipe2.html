Kaju Masala

Ingredients

  • 200 grams Cashews (kaju)
  • 2 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1 cup Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
  • 1 cup Water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

Prepare the Kaju Masala:

  1. Soak cashews in warm water for 15-20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt; cook until the oil separates.
  6. Blend the soaked cashews into a smooth paste, then add it to the pan.
  7. Pour in water and mix well, bringing to a gentle simmer. Adjust water for desired consistency.
  8. Finish with garam masala and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
