Veg Kofta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Grated Bottle Gourd (lauki)
  • 1 cup Boiled Potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup Grated Paneer
  • 1/4 cup Corn Flour
  • 2 Green Chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger Paste
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for frying
  • Chopped Coriander Leaves, for garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Kofta:

  1. In a large bowl, mix grated bottle gourd, mashed potatoes, grated paneer, corn flour, green chilies, ginger paste, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Knead the mixture well until combined and form small balls (koftas).
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and deep fry the koftas until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Make the Gravy:

  1. In another pan, heat oil and sauté 1 finely chopped onion until golden brown.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Stir in 1 cup pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt; cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add 1 cup water and bring to a simmer, then add the koftas to the gravy.
  5. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves before serving.
