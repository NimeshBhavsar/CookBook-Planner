Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200-250 grams Paneer
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • 2 Green Cardamoms
  • 1 cup (120 grams) Onion, cubed
  • 300-350 grams Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander powder (optional)
  • 15 whole Raw cashew nuts
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup Water (for blending)
  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger garlic paste
  • ½ teaspoon Red chili powder (for color, optional)
  • 2-3 tablespoons Cream
  • 1 teaspoon Kasuri methi

Instructions

Prepare the Gravy:

  1. Optional: If using store-bought paneer, soak paneer cubes in hot water for 15-20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add green cardamoms and cubed onions, and sauté until they turn transparent (about 4 minutes).
  3. Add finely chopped tomatoes and salt; sauté for 2-3 minutes, then cover and cook until mushy.
  4. Add Kashmiri red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cashew nuts, and sugar; sauté on low heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Cool the mixture and blend it with 1 cup of water to a smooth puree. Strain if needed.

Make Paneer Butter Masala:

  1. In a pan, melt butter on low flame. Add spices: green cardamoms, bay leaf, cinnamon, and cloves.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the onion-tomato puree and add red chili powder for color, if desired.
  4. Mix well and add 1 cup of water to achieve a gravy consistency; cook until thick and flavorful.
  5. Once the gravy is ready, add paneer cubes and kasuri methi. Stir gently.
  6. Cover and cook for 1-3 minutes, then add cream and mix well for a thick creamy texture.
